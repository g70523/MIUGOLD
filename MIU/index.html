About The Creator

Louise Whitney was born in Quezon City, Philippines and moved to the United States at the age of four years old. Louise's parents were both in the military which caused them to move around a lot. At the age of 13, her family finally settled down in Milwaukee, Wisconsin where her family still currently reside. As Louise got older she learned to enjoy traveling and going on family vacations. Louise's experience with the preparation process of a vacation is the foundation for the application, Suitcase Essentials.

As many of us can relate, Louise found she was always forgetting something each and every time she packed for a trip. Whether is be a toothbrush, or worse, undergarments. So she decided to come up with an application that you can use to help you as you anxiously pack for your upcoming get away.

This application has general packing lists to help you to pack for trips or vacations as short as overnight to as long as two weeks. This portion does not require that a user register for an account. To further customize your own packing list, a user may register himself/herself and create a packing list customizable to what their particular packing needs may be.
